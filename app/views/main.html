SNAPPLR - Help & About

Snapplr is a little application built to show off the concept of exploring and attaching things to features in your immediate vicinity. Eventually, you will be able to attach pictures, playlists, events and statuses to buildings, streets and other geographic features, and discover what others like by just being in the vicinity to the same features (e.g. the other side of the same building). It has been built by @peterneubauer and @skywy as part of the Malm√∂ Music Hack Day during 12h, with help from @brissmyr and @jimtegel.

Thank you!

Source code

Snapplr is developed using the Neo4j Graph Database with the Neo4j Spatial plugin, hosted at Graphene.com and the Userbin Rapid Application framework.

  • click on the map to see Open Street Map Buildings and Ways with their nearest edge closer than 10m to you
  • Log in with Github in order to be able to save you starred buildings and streets
  • Being logged-in, click on a place, then click on a highlighted feature in order to star it.
  • Hit Actions->Reset to reset your starred features.
  • Zoom out to see the bounds of the feature-populated area for this demo.
